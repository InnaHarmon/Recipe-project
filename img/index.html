The Perfect Pb&J

Description

This peanut butter and jelly sandwich is the best one as long as you follow these steps. Results may vary.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of Bread
  • 1 Jar of Jelly
  • 1 Jar of Peanut Butter
  • A Butter knife
  • A Sharp Knife

Directions

  1. Lay both slices of bread next to each other on plate
  2. With butter knife spread layer of jelly on one slice of bread
  3. Spread layer of peanut butter on the other slice of bread
  4. Carefully plzce two slices of bread on top of each other
  5. With a sharp knife, carefully tri, crust from bread.
  6. Cut sandwich diagonally
  7. Enjoy the PB&J!
